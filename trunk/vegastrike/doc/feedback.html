Feedback
    You want to give us some feedback?  Great!  We appreciate feedback of any type or form.  We will take whatever you offer to help us imporve the quality and playability of Vegastrike.  Send any comments to vegastrike-users@lists.sourceforge.net .  Without feedback, we have absolutely no idea what you think about our project, or the direction it is taking, so anything at all will be greatly appreciated!

    Feel free to comment on any aspect of the project, the webpage, the game, the documentation quality.  And please, do not hold back!  There is nothing worse that getting support that you do not deserve (apart from criticism that you don't deserve ;-)
