Default Loadout : 24 Capship Missle, 24 Capship Cluster, 54 Advanced Torpedo
Turrets : 6 capship-light, 4 Heavy, 12 Medium
Default : 6 Flak, 4 Heavy-Defender, 12 Medium-Defender
Info :
In the days before the discovery of Aera, Missile Cruisers were the backbone of confed fleet. They were widely used for police, rescue and peacekeeping operations and played key role in several armed conflicts. But after losing 14 in first weeks of war they were considered outdated. A commission, that was formed to find a way to upgrade them discovered that it is faster and cheaper to build new cruisers, than to refit old ones. So the only advised change was to replace their small turret mountpoints with medium or heavy ones and add Flak turrets. Alltrough they are still called cruisers the jump in military technology forsed Terran command to use them as medium covertes. They can't stand in close combat against most modern capships, however their huge missile load makes them exelent for long range firesupport, and the large number of short range turrets is a doom for enemy fighters. But their weak shields and not so strong armor make this ships favorite target for enemy bombers, so fighter escort is advised.
With a special order of the space command limited number of those was given to several privateers with class A access level.

Info :
The artefacts found in the dead Rlaan colony, discovered by Ogre task force on Taranis during their retreat from Hubble system in first days of the war leaded to the invention of many new technologies with military appliance. They allowed to change the standard Missile Cruiser design and build on it's base a new type of capital ship. With it's highly reinforced hull and mutch heavier armor and shields the ship's mass grew many times and even with the new powerful engines it suffered great drawback in speed and agility. To partially compensate this it's missile load was decreased 3 times. Instead it was armed with long range heavy guns. It also is the first ship to be equipped with the newly discovered long range beams, part of them mounted on turrets. Also some of the medium turrets are replaced with heavy. To compensate the decreased fighter defense two additional flak guns and a missile launcher are added. The resulting ships are strong enough to be a match for most of the Aera capital ships and showed very good survivability rato.
