  • Orbit - a free Space Flight Simulator
  • SpaceThing - a Space Fight Simulator just like mine
  • Mote - a Space Fight Simulator with a Story behind it
  • Foundation - a Space Fight Simulator with a Trading GUI
  • TuxFleet - my own 3D Space Fight Sim based on plib, now obsolete thanks to Vegastrike :-)
