There are two different types of modes or ways to play Vegastrike:
 

Exploration

Your task is it to fly around through the universe, using jump points to jump from system to system and using time compression to able to cross the distance between the jump points.

You can explore new star systems, enjoy the look&feel, fly from planet to planet

You should explore the occasional asteroid field - sometimes, there's a jumpoint or a secret hidden in the middle of the asteroid field.

Enemy and friendly ships will jump in from time to time while you're exploring - you can try to leave them alone, but some enemies won't let you.

To play a exploration-style mission, use any of the missions that are named
    explore_universe*

When you quit an exploration mission with ESC, the system you're in and your position will be saved into your home dir at ~/.vegastrike/save

When you start the exploration mission again, you will resume flight in the system/at the position you quitted.

When you die, the game isn't saved.
 
 

Battle

Battle mission are specially programmed missions where your task is it to fight the enemies and improve your space-superiority fighting skills.

In a battle mission you usually have to peform a special task - like escorting a fleet to their jumppoint, defending you station against enemies or a bombing run on an enemy cruiser.

Currently, all missions that aren't named explore_universe* are battle missions.

When playing a battle mission, have a close look on your message display.
There you'll see when new ships are launched.
And there will be messages from your space command and other radio messages to read.

Please contact us if you have got good ideas for battling missions.
