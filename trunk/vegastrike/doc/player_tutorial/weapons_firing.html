Vegastrike Player Tutorial - Weapons and Firing    to Index

Use those keys to select weapons:
 

  • ENTER     fire missile
  • space      Fire  Guns/Lasers/Beams
  • m             change  active missile
  • g             change  active gun


You can see which guns you are using on the left VDU display in weapon mode:

    here you can see that the middle gun is selected - is it in brackets

The number of weapons, and if they're firing together is dependent on the type of the ships.

Some ships have more weapons, some fire together, some fire seperately.

On some ships you can't change weapons or missiles.
