In the Vegastrike universe, the following factions exist:

  • neutral
    neutral ships are neutral to any other faction
  • confed
    the 'good guys' - the earthlings
    confed is at war with the aera
    confed is slightly suspicious about rlaan ships
  • aera
    the 'bad boys' - the bad alien race with evil in their mind
    they are at war with anyone
  • rlaan
    some other unknown alien race?
    at least, they're in war with the aera, too - but they wouldn't mind a small fight with confed fighters
  • unknown
    well, they're unknown, what can I say?
    just blow'em away, cause they don't like anyone

If you are curious, have a look at the file data/factions.xml - you'll find the factions definitions there.
