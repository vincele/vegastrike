The vssetup tool can be used to configure Vegastrike for your preferences.

Start it by going to the data dir and type:  ./vssetup
 


 

Above you can see a screenshot of vssetup.

You can configure it - at the moment - in 5 categories:

  • Computer
  • Video
  • Sound
  • Difficulty
  • Experiment
The selected settings are shown in a [ Bracket ]

When you think, your selection matches your need/your computer, press 'Done'

If you want to have your settings more refined, you have to edit your vegastrike.config file per hand.
