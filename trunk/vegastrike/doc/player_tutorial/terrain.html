There's more to Vegastrike than just space - Vegastrike is a space flight sim with a terrain rendering engine included.

To see the terrain mode in Vegastrike, you should best play the mission

    earth_patrol1

or an exporation mission that starts in Sol - or fly to Sol to see it there.

See those screenshots:

 :  :  :  : 

The first one was taken immediately after the start of the 'eart_patrol1' mission, when you've rotated to face earth.

Then you have to accelerate (use the afterburners) and dive down on the planet.

It won't take long and then you'll reach the clouds layer of the earth - in screenshot 2, you're just about the fly through the clouds layer.

Now fly even deeper and deeper, until you think you are crashing into the earth itself - and that's the moment when the terrain will show up like screenshot 3.

You can fly low above the terrain - take your time, fly once around the earth over the terrain - that takes some while, the earth is big - and experience the sun going up over the terrain, see the lighning change from darkness to light (screenshot 4)

If you fly up again, the terrain will vanish and the blue-green earth sphere will be there again.

The hardware requirements to be able to see the terrain are harder than for the rest of Vegastrike.
You'll need at least 128MB RAM and 128MB swap - 256MB RAM might be better.

At present, the terrain is not quite usuable - except as a graphics demonstration and for your enjoyment.
The AIs can't yet fly properly about terrain, and we don't have buildings or planes or helicopters or tanks yet - in code as well as graphics/models.

If you'd like to help us here - by creating 3D models of such vehicles, or by programming new vehicle units and vehicle AIs, we would appreciate - please contact us :-)

Some time, there should be a proper fly-low-over-surface mode with buildings, trees, tanks, cars, planes, helis etc, so that we can also play 'Heli-Sim' or 'Tank-Commander' in Vegastrike :-)
