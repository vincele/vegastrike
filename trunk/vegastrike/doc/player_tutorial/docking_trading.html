Docking:

docking is not implemented in Vegastrike-0.2.0 - only in CVS for now, but coming soon :-)

Your space fighter can dock on stations, mining bases, cruisers etc

These keys are used with docking:

  • C    request clearance

    • press C to request docking clearance from the station
  • d     dock

    • dock your ship
  • u     undock
related:
  • home        match velocity

    • your flight computer tries to match to velocity of your targeted ship
    velocity means speed AND direction
    TODO: explain that better
  • end           stop match velocity


To dock to a station, you have to target the station and then fly near the docking bays and come to a standstill.
Then press C to request clearance to land from your station.
and then press d to dock to dock. Press u to undock.

If you're at the wrong spot, you won't be able to dock.
